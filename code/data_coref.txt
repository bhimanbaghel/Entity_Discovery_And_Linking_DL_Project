Narendra Damodardas Modi is an Indian politician who is the 16th and current Prime Minister of India, in office since May 2014.
Narendra Damodardas Modi was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014, and is the Member of Parliament for Varanasi.
Narendra Damodardas Modi, a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), is a Hindu nationalist and member of the right-wing Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (the right-wing Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh).
